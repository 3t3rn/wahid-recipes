Salad Recipe

Fresh and Healthy Salad

A salad is a dish consisting of a mixture of small pieces of food, usually vegetables or fruit.
Salads are typically served at room temperature or chilled, and they can be served as an appetizer,
side dish, or main course.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups mixed greens (lettuce, spinach, arugula)
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/4 cup olives (black or green)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine the mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, bell pepper, and red onion.
  2. Add the feta cheese and olives to the bowl.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, balsamic vinegar, salt, and pepper.
  4. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss gently to combine.
  5. Serve immediately or refrigerate for up to 30 minutes before serving.
